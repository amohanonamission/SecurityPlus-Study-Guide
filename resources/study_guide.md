# Security+ Study Guide (Detailed Tips)

## 1. Study Philosophy
- Immerse yourself **curiously**, not just for the exam.  
- **Understand concepts**, don’t just memorize answers.  
- Focus on **PBQs** — they are often the hardest part.  
- Use **spaced repetition**: review weak topics every 2–3 days.  
- Track **progress** using a checklist of exam objectives.

---

## 2. Labs & PBQs
- Always simulate attacks in a **virtual lab**.  
- Use **Kali Linux + Windows VMs** to test firewall rules, permissions, and malware simulations.  
- Document every attempt to reinforce learning.  
- Repeat labs until you can do them **without notes**.  

### Mini Lab Ideas
1. Configure **firewall rules** and test access.  
2. Setup **MFA for remote users**.  
3. Simulate **file integrity monitoring** and alerting.  
4. Generate a **digital signature** and verify it.
